The Backrooms | Complete Guide?
The Backrooms. Some say that the Backrooms are just fictional. Some others
completely believe in the Backrooms. So, what are the Backrooms even are? The
Backrooms are a liminal reality with complete dread and isolation. The Backrooms have 8
confirmed levels and over 8000 unconfirmed levels! The levels are divided into 5
categories/clusters
Cluster I: It contains The Main Nine Levels until level 999
Cluster II: It contains the levels 1000-1999
Cluster III: Levels 2000-2999
Cluster IV: Contains Levels 3000-3999
Cluster V+: Contains Levels 4000-8000+
The Backrooms also contain entities. Entities will attempt to murder you to send you
into level 3004 in the Fourth Cluster. The reason has stayed unknown. Entities are classified by
four categories: their number, name, hazard, and habitat. "Number", as the name suggests,
refers to the numerical notation of an entity. "Name" is what the entity is known as. "Hazard"
refers to the implicit danger of an entity, ranked on a scale of zero (0) to seven (7). Lastly,
"habitat" refers to where an entity typically resides.
Entity Hazard Classes
Class 0: No threats posed whatsoever
Class 1: Not aggressive but can cause injuries to wanderers.
Class 2: Provokable and can injure wanderers.
Class 3: Aggressive but weak.
Class 4: Aggressive. (Typical classification level)
Class 5: Dangerous.
Class 6: Extremely dangerous.
Class 7: Mythological. (Classification level for unknown/anomalous entities)
Entity Habitats
Unknown: Their location is unknown.
Extinct: The entity has been wiped out.
Nearly Extinct: The entity is almost wiped out and remaining are hidden in
levels.
Rare: Found on less than 5 levels.
Common: Found on more than 20 Levels.
Majority: Found on over 50% of levels.
Many: Found on at least a dozen levels.
The Backrooms: Found in all levels.

How to enter the Backrooms
To enter the Backrooms, one must "noclip" out of reality. There isn't a guaranteed method of
achieving this, as it occurs inconsistently. However, there are some tips to increase the chances
of entering the Backrooms. One method is to find an "entrance". These are glitches that lead to
the Backrooms, but they occur extremely rarely. Such entrances include:
Walls that are a shade darker than normal, or that do not appear to cast a shadow.
Doors that did not exist previously and appear to be out-of-place.
Areas that give a sense of unease, where one feels strangely offput.
On the extremely rare chance that you have found an entrance to the Backrooms, do not
approach it unless you are properly prepared.
If you happen to accidentally stumble into the Backrooms, the first thing you must do is stay
calm.
The necessary supplies to bring for the first excursions are: First aid kit, Backpack, Weapon,
Timer or watch, Canteen or water bottle, Food source, preferably one that will not expire
quickly, Sleeping bag, Durable spare clothing, Flashlight, or headlamp
NOTE: Backrooms will be your new home. DO NOT GO ON EXIT SIGNS! It is probably a trap.
